## Suggested Naming Conventions & Tiers (new)

To position this as a single, unified platform rather than two separate products, we need cohesive naming for the modules and the subscription tiers. 

**The Platform Modules:**
* **OmniDesk (formerly Chat/Chatwoot):** Your Omnichannel Support Desk. Features live chat, unified inboxes, community connection, lead capture, and a centralized knowledge base.
* **SocialStudio (formerly Social/Postiz):** Your Marketing Department. Offers social media management, audience building, lead generation, and omnichannel publishing. 

**The Subscription Tiers:**
* **Growth Tier (2-3 seats):** Targeted at Creators, Influencers, and Mid-Size Merchants. Provides the full suite of growth and orchestrating tools with a lean headcount.
* **Scale Tier (Unlimited seats/workspaces):** Targeted at Agencies and large teams. Offers unlimited team members, agents, and multi-tenant client workspaces.

## Universal Context

All personas share one macro-problem: they're running real, sophisticated digital businesses using tools that were never designed to work together. The fragmentation isn't just a skill gap — it's a structural ceiling on their growth. 

Three universal gaps cut across every persona:

* **The Conversation Chaos Tax** — Messages drowning across Instagram DMs, WhatsApp, Facebook, email simultaneously. No unified view. Things fall through.
* **The Content Consistency Struggle** — Posting reactively, inconsistently, out of guilt. No system, just recurring catch-up.
* **The Migration & Scaling Friction (new)** — They are already paying for fragmented freemium or premium tools (like Buffer) but are terrified of the operational downtime required to migrate to a better system.

2. **Never miss a lead or a message.** Every channel, one inbox. Use omnichannel live chat to connect with your community anywhere, all from one place.
3. **Content that actually builds an audience (new).** Plan weeks ahead from a single view. Capture leads, grow your audience, and sell your goodies without the reactive panic.
4. **Scale without friction (new).** Start with the Growth Tier to consolidate your operations, and seamlessly move to the Scale Tier (with unlimited seats) when you are ready to build an empire.
