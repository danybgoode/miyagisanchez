## Persona B — The Digital Influencer / Content Creator
**Role in strategy:** Distribution channel AND customer. NOW market.

**Who they are:** Micro to mid-tier influencers (10K–500K followers) looking to productize their services and audience. They use tools like Buffer and are highly accustomed to the digital environment. 

**Tagline (new):** "This is how you scale to millions."

**Jobs to be done:**
* **Audience Management at Scale (new):** Transition from manually replying to followers to managing audience interactions as a scalable, productized operation.
* Maintain consistent publishing presence across multiple channels to build an audience and capture leads faster **(new)**.
* Manage brand deal conversations, collab requests, and fan interactions through a unified omnichannel desk **(new)**.

**Pains:**
* **Migration Anxiety (new):** They want to upgrade from basic schedulers like Buffer but dread the friction of moving their established (if messy) workflows.
* DMs from followers, brand inquiries, and collabs pile up with no system — it all looks the same.
* Managing 3–4 content channels manually while also managing inbound communications is cognitively exhausting.

**Aspirations & Emotional Layer (new):** They want to transition from "creator" to "media empire." They aspire to scale their influence to millions without burning out, leveraging seamless infrastructure to look incredibly professional to brand sponsors. 

**Primary Tier:** Growth Tier (2-3 seats).

**Value proposition for them:** This is how you scale to millions. Ditch the friction of your old scheduler and migrate seamlessly to a unified platform. Use **SocialStudio** to grow your audience and publish everywhere at once, while **OmniDesk** acts as your personal support center for brand deals and community engagement. Hypothesis to test:Persona B (Digital Influencer): They will overcome migration anxiety if the platform promises to scale their brand deals and audience management professionally
