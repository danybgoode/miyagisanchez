01 · About

Mexico is a
great market.
Its tax code
is not.

You chose Mexico because of the talent, the market size, the proximity to the US. Nobody told you about CFDI, SAT, ISR, IVA, régimen fiscal, declaración anual, and the particular joy of the DIOT.

We're the team that bridges that gap. 20+ years in product and technology leadership, MSc Finance from a UK university, and deep roots in the Mexican regulatory landscape.

We don't replace your finance function — we give it superpowers it didn't know were possible.

Every client gets a dedicated lead who speaks fluent English and fluent SAT. We're available via WhatsApp, we think in unit economics, and we've never once said "seleccione la opción 1."

03 · Methodology

Finance meets
product thinking.

Most accounting firms tell you what happened. We connect what happened financially to what happened in your product — and show you what it means for what happens next.

  • 01
    North Star alignment. Your financial model should reflect your product's core metric. We build P&L structures that map to how your product actually creates value.
  • 02
    Unit economics that make sense. CAC, LTV, payback period — connected to your actual Mexican cost structure, not a US template.
  • 03
    Cost of opportunity, not compliance theatre. SAT compliance isn't a cost — it's access to financial instruments, credit, and investor confidence. We show you the math.
  • 04
    Quarterly strategy sessions. Not a report dump. A real conversation about where your business is going and what the numbers are telling you.
